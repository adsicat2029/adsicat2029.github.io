Gather Your Materials!

A good crocheter always knows what they need. Start strong by collecting all the materials you need.

If you don't know yet, you can buy a 4.5mm or 5.0mm hook, at least 3 balls of yarn, and a pair of scissors.

On Hook Handling

Pick with which you're most comfortable with. The two most common are the "knife" and "pencil" hold.

Making a Slipknot

Create a small loop, insert your hook and pull the yarn going to the ball you're using. With the hook still in the loop, pull both ends and voila, there's your slipknot!

Yarn Holding

Wrap your yarn around the pinky, then lead it back to the front of your index, then wrap it around again.

Chaining

Insert your hook into the slipknot and pull the yarn from the ball (this is called yarning over). After this you should have one chain! Keep chaining until you reach your desired length.
